Leadership



VIc Cekvenich—Co-founder and CEO: Vic is a seasoned tech executive and entrepreneur with a proven track record of success. He has over thirty years experience combining a strong technical background and business leadership. He believes that technology must be developed solely to achieve business objectives and to satisfy market demands. `I believe innovation happens with small and elite teams of superstars rather than with armies of mediocre players. I also believe that creating an environment of high innovation requires a culture of high-output based on merit; a place where people are rewarded for taking good risks.`

Hon Wong—Chairman of the Board: Hon is a successful founder, executive, angel investor, and active board member of technology start-ups. He has led multiple start-ups through their entire life-cycle from conception and growth to exits through IPOs and M&As. Hon has extensive hands-on experience with all aspects of the start-up process. He has worked with and guided many management teams through the challenges faced by all technology start-ups. These include fund raising, team building, competitive positioning, market and partnership development, pivoting, and go to market strategy formation and execution. Hon has worked with enterprise software, SaaS, cloud infrastructure and manufacturing service companies.

Fadel Darwish—Co-founder and VP of Business Development: Fadel is a prolific, hands-on, high-grit builder and problem solver with 9+ years of experience managing $70 million engineering projects and business ventures. Experienced in business development, business modeling, and go-to market strategies.

Toshiaki Takada—VP of Engineering: Toshiaki is an executive level product leader with over twenty years hands-on software development experience and twelve years of operational experience in internet, working with core protocols, IP routers and networking applications. He enjoys leading high performance teams in all phases of innovation-based products from designing, developing and sustaining products on various platforms and applications. His depth and scope of knowledge, hard-work and team work helps team members excel in their job.

Shamik Mehta, Advisor: Shamik has been and Angel Investor since 2001 with over 25 years of sales, marketing, business development, and technical experience in high tech software industry. Prior to that was head of Global Sales for TIBCO for the finance market where he was instrumental in growing revenue by 40x in 12 years.

About Us
